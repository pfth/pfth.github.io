Who are we?

Points for the Homeless, Inc. is dedicated to providing basic necessities to members of society that are in need or going through hardships. This includes, but is not limited to, homeless, disabled and mentally impaired people, as well as single mothers, veterans and those working part-or full-time but still living in poverty.
