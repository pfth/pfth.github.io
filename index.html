We believe in one cultural - the human culture.

To preserve our culture, it is our obligation to assure that every member of society has access to basic necessities. Here at Points for the Homeless, we developed pH - an app that empowers and incentivize all members of society to fight poverty, hunger, and homelessness.

Who We Are

Points for the Homeless is dedicated to providing basic necessities to members of society that are in need or going through hardships. This includes, but is not limited to, homeless, disabled and mentally impaired people, as well as single mothers, veterans and those working part-or full-time but still living in poverty.

How We Do It

The pH app allows users to purchase basic necessities for pH members. A pH member is an individual that is registered with the company and can be anyone experiencing malnourishment, homelessness and/or living in poverty. pH members have pH identification numbers (pHIDs) that allow users to purchase items to a specific pH member of choice.
