How do we do it?


pH is a mobile application and web portal that empowers members of all classes and levels of society with a tool to make a difference in lives of others through innovative, impactful and meaningful purchases.

The pH app allows users to purchase basic necessities for pH members. A pH member is an individual that is registered with the company and can be anyone experiencing malnourishment, homelessness and/or living in poverty. pH members have pH identification numbers (pHIDs) that allow users to purchase items to a specific pH member of choice.

This provides the means by which everyday people can make a positive impact on the lives of pH members that are in need.
